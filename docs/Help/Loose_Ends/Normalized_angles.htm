Normalized angles

Phase-related properties may be expressed as normalized angles. A normalized angle maps the full range of angles (360 degrees) to the range zero to one. The following table shows the relationship between normalized angles and degrees:

Values outside the range [0..1] wrap around, e.g. 1.25 wraps to 90° and −0.25 wraps to 270°.
